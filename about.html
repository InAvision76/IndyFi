Indy-Fi is a sci-fi brand that presents thought-provoking visions from independent filmmakers. From feature films to short films, series and podcasts, INDY-FI works with emerging voices globally. Our library of films consists of more than 500 action-drama stories.

Recent projects include the SXSW 2018 feature film Immersion, the Youtube Watch series “Anica”, and short film Above The Ground starring Shahroze Khan. INDY-FI will also be premiering the new series, "Detrix", based off the 2009 popular webseries, "Anica", produced and directed by S.K. Lawless.

New series “Fallen” and "Thalam" won Best Sci-fi Drama in its premiere at Tribeca 2019, and "The Man On The Inside", produced by Landon Ayers & S.K. Lawless.

Having launched in December 2020, INDY-FI is the most effecient stream network with current and upcoming distribution on Roku, DirecTV, and Hulu and more. In addition, has partnered with Tribeca, Hollywood Film Fest, Houston Film Fest and more, winners have showcased their work and moved on to major contracts with established studios.

Currently, INDY-FI is watched by more than 4M people across the globe, streaming sci-fi content in throughout the world. Giving a new voice for the independent filmmaker.
